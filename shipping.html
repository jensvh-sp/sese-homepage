Problems With Your Delivery?

Damaged parcel: Email customerservice@sese.be with your order number and a photo of the damage. We'll resolve it quickly.

Wrong product received: Contact us within 48 hours of receipt. We'll arrange the correct product for you.

Parcel not received: First check your track & trace link. If the issue persists, contact us and we'll investigate with the carrier.

05
Good to Know

Address changes are possible before dispatch — contact us as soon as possible after placing your order.

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch. Once dispatched, you'll need to use our returns process.

No customs fees — all orders ship from within the EU, so there are no additional import duties for EU customers.
