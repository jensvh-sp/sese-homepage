01
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, to improve functionality, and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies do not harm your device or contain personal information such as passwords or payment details. They are used to recognise your browser and remember certain information about your visit.

This policy explains what cookies sese.be uses, why we use them, and how you can control them. It applies in accordance with the Belgian/EU ePrivacy Directive (transposed in Belgian law via the Law of 13 June 2005 on electronic communications) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

02
Types of Cookies We Use
Strictly Necessary Always active
These cookies are essential for the website to function. They enable core features such as secure checkout, shopping cart persistence, session management, and language preferences. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly. No consent is required for strictly necessary cookies under the ePrivacy Directive.
Functional Optional
These cookies remember your preferences to provide a more personalised experience. For example, your chosen language (NL / EN / FR) and any display preferences. They do not track your browsing activity on other websites.
Analytical Optional
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with sese.be — which pages are visited, where visitors come from, and how they move through the site. Data collected is anonymised or pseudonymised. We use this information to improve the website's content and structure. Provided by tools such as Google Analytics, if active.
Marketing Consent required
Marketing cookies track your browsing activity to display relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness. These are only placed with your explicit consent. You can opt out at any time through the cookie consent banner or your browser settings.
03
Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on sese.be are placed by third parties. These include:

  • Shopify: Our e-commerce platform sets cookies necessary for cart functionality, checkout, and fraud prevention. Shopify's cookie policy can be found at shopify.com/legal/cookies.
  • Google Analytics (if active): Used to collect anonymised information about site usage. You can opt out of Google Analytics across all websites using Google's opt-out browser add-on (tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
  • Payment providers: Payment processors may set cookies necessary to verify transactions and prevent fraud.

We do not control third-party cookies directly. Please refer to the respective third-party privacy policies for more information.

04
How to Manage Cookies

When you first visit sese.be, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can accept or decline optional cookies at that point. You can also change your preferences at any time.

Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to control and delete cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the functionality of sese.be, including the ability to add items to your cart or complete a purchase.
