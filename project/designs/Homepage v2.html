Is SESE suitable for sensitive skin? +
Yes. Every SESE formula is fragrance-free, built around well-tolerated actives, and developed to support the skin barrier rather than challenge it. Several products carry the Allergen-Label-Free certification.
When will I see results? +
Hydration is often noticeable on the first morning. Visible improvements in texture, radiance and firmness typically appear within 2–4 weeks of consistent daily use.
Can I use the Peptide and Retinol Alternative serums together? +
Yes — they're built to complement each other. Peptide Serum in the morning supports firmness; Retinol Alternative at night supports gentle renewal. Layer with a moisturiser in between.
How long does delivery take? +
Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days from Belgium and arrive within 2–5 business days across Europe. Shipping is free on orders over €60.
Can I return my order? +
Yes. EU law gives you 14 days to return any unopened, unused product, and SESE adds an extra 30-day satisfaction guarantee on top — no questions asked.
Are the products vegan and natural? +
All SESE products are vegan and cruelty-free. The majority are COSMOS Natural certified, with clean formulas free of phthalates, parabens and synthetic fragrance.
